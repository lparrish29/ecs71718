My Achievements









Throughout my life, I have been in a range of clubs and sports such as soccer, cross country, track and field, choir and chess. From first grade to fourth grade I was in the chess club at my school. Each year, I placed in the top five for my age group in tournaments, and in fourth grade I won. I started doing running sports when I was in seventh grade, and I competed in shotput, 100m dash, and 4x100 relay. It wasn't until 8th grade though that I won 4th in shotput for varsity, and my relay team got 1st place at our meet. In sixth grade, I won the invention convention at my school, where students had to find a problem and create a solution. Academically, I have been accepted to Lane Tech College Prep. I am in Alpha, which is a rigorous science and math based program, and have become a Lane Scholar.
